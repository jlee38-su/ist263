Popular Pork Cuts

1. Sam-Gyeob-Sal (삼겹살) - Pork Belly

Pork Belly is the most popular cut of pork in Korean BBQ. The distinctive feature of the cut is that it has layers of fat and meat which makes it very succulent. Because it is a fatty cut, it needs to be grilled until well-done. You would definitely want to choose this cut if you are a first-timer because it is easy to grill.

2. Hang-Jeong-Sal (항정살) - Pork Neck

Pork Neck is another fatty cut of pork in Korean BBQ. The distinctive feature of the cut is that it has similar marbling of beef. Also, unlike other cuts, it has a little bit of a flaky texture. This cut is also very fatty, so it needs to be grilled until well-done.

3. Mok-Sal (목살) - Pork Blade

Pork Blade is a famous cut that has less fat than the two cuts shown above. The distinctive feature of this cut is that it has both soft and chewy textures. This cut is preferred by people who dislike the fat on the meat. This cut should be grilled to medium.
