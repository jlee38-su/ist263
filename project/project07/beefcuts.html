Popular Beef Cuts

1. Salchi Sal (살치살) - Beef Chuck Flap Tail

Chuck Flap Tail is one of the most famous cuts in Korea. It is famous for being the fattiest meat in Korean BBQ. The distinctive feature of this cut is that its marbling ratio is very high. Sometimes there is more fat than meat. This cut should be grilled until medium.

2. An-Chang-Sal (안창살) - Beef Outside Skirt

Outside Skirt is one of the most expensive cuts in Korea due to its scarcity. It is famous for having a beefy and flavorful taste. Due to its distinct flavor, this cut is not for everyone but it is highly recommended to try. This cut should be grilled until medium-rare.

3. Chadol-Baegi (차돌박이) - Brisket

Brisket is also famous in Korea and sometimes small portions of the cut are served as complimentary. Brisket can be very chewy so it is served in very thin slices. Because it is so thin, you only have to grill couple of seconds on each side.
